Owner Tips

Handy tips as a generator owner

A few things to keep in mind to get the most out of your investment.

Listen for weekly tests

Your generator should run every week on the same day and at the same time. If it is not, please call our office.

Weekly testing is not optional

Run time may vary. Daylight savings time affects the schedule when seasons change. If your generator is not running weekly, check first for error codes then call us for next steps.

To lock or not to lock

All generators come with a key to lock the top. This key is small and easily lost. Consider leaving it unlocked or marking your key and keeping it somewhere you'll easily remember.

Extended outage oil top-off

If your unit runs for 48 hours straight during a power outage you will need to top off oil. Use 5W30 full synthetic oil from any local auto parts store. Turn off your unit, wait ten minutes, then add oil.

✋

Stop — before you call

If your generator needs attention or has an error code, take a look at our website first for quick reference guides and easy-to-follow videos. Doing so may save you the cost of a service call. Visit powerhousegenerator.com
